Why Choose Us?


We're your one-stop shop for daily life accessories, offering a curated collection of high-quality items at prices that won't break the bank. to make your everyday life easier and more stylish.

We believe everyone deserves stylish and functional accessories without the premium price tag. We source high quality materials and carefully design each bag to be your perfect everyday companion.

We use eco-friendly dyes and sustainable manufacturing processes to minimize our impact on the planet.

About Us

We started this business because we were tired of flimsy, uninspired tote bags. We wanted to create bags that were not only beautiful and functional but also reflected our commitment to sustainability and ethical production. We believe in creating products that are good for people and the planet. That's why we use organic cotton, recycled materials, and partner with fair-trade suppliers. Each bag is carefully designed and handcrafted in our studio using high-quality materials. We pay attention to every detail, from the stitching to the finishing touches.

I'm VAISHNAVI, the founder and designer of TOTE BAG. I'm passionate about creating beautiful and functional bags that people will love. Our vision is to become a leading provider of sustainable and stylish tote bags that empower people to make conscious choices.
